Privacy Policy for Cubzo

Last updated: June 21, 2026

Cubzo does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Cubzo is designed for children ages 4–5 and is fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Information We Collect

Cubzo does not collect any personal information from users. We do not request, access, or store names, email addresses, photos, location data, contacts, or any other personal or identifying information.

Data Storage

Any pictures your child creates within the app are saved only on your device. This data never leaves the device, is never transmitted to us or any third party, and is not accessible by us.

Third-Party Services

Cubzo does not include any third-party advertising, analytics, tracking, or social media services. The app works fully offline and does not connect to any external servers.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Cubzo is intended for young children and is designed in full compliance with COPPA. Because we collect no personal information of any kind, no parental consent is required for data collection — there is simply no data being collected.

In-App Purchases

Cubzo may offer optional in-app purchases to unlock additional content. These purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store any payment information. All purchases are protected behind a parental gate.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
